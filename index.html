Car Performance

The Chevrolet Corvette impresses on all performance fronts. It accelerates up to speed and carves around turns with remarkable ease, and its absorbent suspension ensures a fairly comfortable ride, even on rougher roads. It also gets decent fuel economy for a high-powered V8 sports car.

The 2023 Chevrolet Corvette furthers its reputation as a high-value everyday supercar. The 'Vette provides blistering acceleration, phenomenal handling, a comfortable and well-trimmed cabin and usable cargo space, yet it costs far less than its exotic sparring partners.
